What is Price Alert?
🇬🇧 English

Price Alert is a script mod for Farming Simulator 25 that adds a complete price monitoring system integrated directly into the in-game menu. Never miss the right moment to sell your products or animals again.

  • Tracks stock levels and selling prices for all product types: silos, production buildings, bales, pallets, and goods loaded on vehicles and trailers. Animals are monitored with breed-specific logic, also displaying age in months and health status
  • Four tabs: Products in Stock, Products with Active Alert, Unsellable Products, All Products
  • Configure custom alert thresholds for each product or animal breed: minimum quantity in stock and minimum price percentage relative to the best possible price
  • Products that have reached their configured thresholds blink in the menu. Two types of in-game alerts are available: a non-invasive notification that appears without interrupting gameplay, and an invasive popup that stops fast-forward and sleep mode, restoring time to normal speed (1x)
  • View all storage locations, enclosures and selling points for each product or animal, with map hotspot marking. The best selling point is highlighted, but all available ones are accessible
  • Supports 7 languages: English, Italian, German, French, Polish, Spanish, Portuguese
⚠ This version supports single player only.

The Author
🇬🇧 English

I'm Redentore, an old school gamer born in 1981 who discovered the joy of modding with Farming Simulator 25. Price Alert is my first mod — after putting a lot of work into it, I decided to distribute it independently rather than through official channels, because I believe in keeping things simple and direct.

I enjoy building things and seeing them come to life — whether it's a mod, a website, or anything else. That's why I built this page from scratch instead of using existing platforms.

Regarding the official GIANTS ModHub: I did submit the mod, but after multiple rejections during the pre-screening phase — with feedback so vague it was nearly impossible to understand what actually needed fixing — I decided to take a different path. Working in an advanced technical support helpdesk, I know firsthand how important clear, actionable feedback is when someone is trying to solve a problem. The mod works: in-game testing shows zero errors and zero warnings, and it passed the official GIANTS TestRunner successfully.
